# Tracker Dumper

**XMODITS**

Dump samples from popular tracker formats!


## Supported Formats:
|Format| 8-bit| 16-bit| compression|Stereo|
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
|IT| ✓|✓|✓|N/A|
| XM  | 🚧| 🚧| 🚧| 🚧|
| S3M | 🚧| 🚧| N/A| 🚧|
| MOD | 🚧| 🚧| 🚧| 🚧|
| UMX | 🚧| 🚧| 🚧| 🚧|


|Key| Meaning|
|---|---|
|**✓** | Fully supported with little or no bugs.|
| **/** | Sorta works|
| **X** | unsupported |
| **n/a** | Format doesn't support it|
| ⏳ | In progress|
| 🚧 | Part of roadmap |
